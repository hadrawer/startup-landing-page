Create a modern, professional landing page for a startup project called “ILM”.

ILM is a local Gulf-based educational platform, designed as a smarter and faster alternative to Blackboard for universities and schools in Bahrain and the GCC.

The platform combines multiple tools into one website instead of forcing users to depend on many separate systems.

Core idea:
ILM is an all-in-one academic platform that includes:
1. A Blackboard alternative for courses, materials, grades, announcements, and academic management.
2. A LockDown Browser alternative for secure online exams.
3. A Microsoft Teams alternative for live classes, meetings, chat, and collaboration.
4. An AI detector tool for doctors and instructors to check student submissions.
5. An AI generator for doctors to create quizzes, assignments, assessments, and midterm exams when needed.

What makes ILM different from normal Blackboard:
- It combines several platforms into one unified system.
- It solves the common problem of Blackboard being slow and frustrating.
- It is locally based in Bahrain, which means faster customer support and quicker issue fixing.
- It understands Gulf universities, students, doctors, and academic needs better.
- It feels closer to the user because it is designed for the local education environment.
- It gives stronger confidence in data privacy because the platform is local and region-focused.

Design direction:
Suggest a completely new and modern design concept. Do not copy Blackboard, Microsoft Teams, or any existing LMS design. The page should feel original, polished, and human-designed, not generic or obviously AI-generated.

Visual style:
- Modern SaaS landing page
- Clean, premium, and trustworthy
- Suitable for education, universities, and government/private institutions
- Use a professional color palette inspired by technology, trust, and the Gulf region
- Avoid childish education visuals
- Use strong spacing, elegant typography, smooth sections, and realistic UI mockups
- The design should feel startup-ready and presentation-ready

Landing page sections:
1. Hero section with a strong headline about replacing outdated academic systems with one faster local platform.
2. Short subheading explaining that ILM combines LMS, secure exams, live classes, AI detection, and AI academic content generation.
3. Call-to-action buttons such as “Request a Demo” and “Explore Features”.
4. Feature section showing the five main platform modules.
5. Problem section explaining why current systems like Blackboard are slow, fragmented, and not locally supported.
6. Solution section explaining how ILM solves these problems.
7. Comparison section showing ILM vs traditional Blackboard.
8. Local trust section focusing on Bahrain, GCC support, faster response, and data privacy.
9. AI tools section explaining the AI detector and AI quiz/assessment generator for doctors.
10. Final CTA section encouraging universities and institutions to try ILM.

Tone:
Professional, confident, clear, and investor-friendly. Avoid exaggerated marketing claims. Keep the text realistic and suitable for a startup pitch or university presentation.

Output:
Generate the full landing page design and content. Include layout suggestions, section structure, headings, short copy, button text, and visual direction. Make it responsive for desktop and mobile.
